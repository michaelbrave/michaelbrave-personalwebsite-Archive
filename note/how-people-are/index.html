We Seek Out What We Lack - In All Areas Of Life, Careers/Love/Hobbies
People often choose careers to fill what was previously missing in their lives

World Class Musicians on Street Corners - We are in Awe of Average Things
A lot of what we are in awe of are really quite common, cheap even and are not worthy of our awe. There are pretty people everywhere, it’s the mathematical average. Talented musicians sit on street corners and play for change,
Similarly we discount our own abilities. My design knowledge can look like superpowers to those not versed in it, but I take it for granted everyday. Even my low level coding skills seem like magic to those who don’t yet know it.
The inverse is also true, sometimes we do not value something incredible because it seems common (like artistic ability) often this has more to do with presentation than content, because everything is only as good as it’s value multiplied by it’s presentation.
