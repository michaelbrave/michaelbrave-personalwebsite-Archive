Who is Mike Brave

I’m what happens when an artist becomes a designer and later a developer.

My lifelong mission (other than finding the perfect lemonaide) is to use technology to make art better, cheaper and faster. Hence my interest in Neural Networks, it’s why I love things like procedural generation, and It’s why I get excited about game engines being used in ways outside of making games.

Other things worth mentioning;

  1. my favorite programming language is Swift (I believe there is something special there)
  2. I collect old electronics that have interesting designs or interfaces (like mp3 players)
  3. I love to read, and can often be found in old bookstores
  4. I travel as often as I can, my favorite trip so far being the Camino de Santiago
  5. My primary non creative hobby is finding and knowing about lesser known restaurants
  6. I’m terrible at keeping in touch with everyone, even my favorite people, appologies in advance
