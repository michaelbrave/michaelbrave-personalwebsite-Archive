About

Who is Mike Brave

I’m what happens when you take an artist, turn them into a designer and then later a developer.

My lifelong mission (other than finding the perfect lemonaide) is to use technology to make better and cheaper art. This explains my interests in Neural Networks (especially being used to create things), it’s why I love things like procedural generation, and It’s why I get excited about game engines being used in ways other than for making games.

Other things worth mentioning; my favorite programming language is Swift (there is something special there), I collect old electronics (like mp3 players), I love to read broadly, to have deep conversations and to travel to places a little off the path. Finding and knowing about hidden restaurants and writing little pieces of wisdom are guilty pleasures of mine. Also I am terrible at keeping in touch with people, even my favorite people.

You can reach me at twitter or linkedin or github.
